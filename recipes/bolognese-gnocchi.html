Bolognese Gnocchi



This quick one pot gnocchi or should I say gnocchi
Bolognese would knock your socks off. Soft,
pillowy gnocchi loaded with tomato sauce, melt
in the mouth ground beef, and other fresh
ingredients. It is simple, easy, and comes
together in about 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Potato Gnocchi
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic paste
  • Hot Sauce
  • Italian seasoning
  • Bay leaves
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Brown Sugar
  • Chopped Tomatoes
  • Salt and black Pepper
  • Water

Steps

  1. Start by sauteing the finely chopped until soft and fragrant.
    This is a good base to every pasta dish or any dish for that matter.

  2. Add ground beef, break into the saut√©ed onion and cook until brown.

  3. Into the browned beef, add tomato puree, garlic paste, hot sauce,
    chicken stock powder, brown sugar, Italian herbs, water,
    salt, and black pepper, and mix to combine

  4. Finally, add the chopped tomatoes, bay leaves, stir to combine,
    and simmer for about 20 minutes. then add the gnocchi,
    stir into the Bolognese sauce and simmer for another 3 to 5 minutes,
    stir in some grated cheese into the Bolognese, take it off the heat and ou are done.
